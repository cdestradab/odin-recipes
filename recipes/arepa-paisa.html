Arepa Paisa

Description

It’s a popular cornmeal flatbread and can be served on its own or topped with eggs, cheese or meat

Ingredients

  • Arepa Flour (Cornmeal)
  • Warm Water (not boiling)
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Start by combining flour, warm water, butter, and salt. Mix well and knead it into a soft dough.
  2. Cut the dough into 8-10 equal portions and shape each into a small ball. Then roll each one out into thin disks.
  3. Add butter to a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook the disks for about 3-5 minutes on each side until the surface turns golden brown.
