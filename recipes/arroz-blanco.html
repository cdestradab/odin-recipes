Arroz Blanco

Description

The Arroz Blanco is a traditional recipe in Colombia, it usually accompanies main dishes and it is also the base for other recipes with rice. It has a lot of variations, but regularly it is expected be dry, and not sticky.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic and onion with a knife
  2. Saute the vegetables with a third part of olive oil for one part of rice, in a pot
  3. Add 2 parts of water for 1 part of rice on the pot and put it on mid fire
  4. Add salt to the water until it tastes like simple broth until it starts boiling
  5. Add the rice to the broth and let it boil until the water evaporates juste enough so the top of the rice is out of the water
  6. Stir the rice carefully so it cooks evenly but do not stir it too much, just one time
  7. Put the fire on the minimum, cover it and let it dry slowly
  8. Be wary to not let it dry too much so it starts to burn on the bottom
  9. Serve hot on the plate
